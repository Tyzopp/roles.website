About Roles

Powerful Discord role management made simple

What is Roles?

Roles is a Discord bot designed to automate and streamline role management in your Discord server. Whether you're running a small community or a large server with thousands of members, Roles helps you manage permissions, assign roles efficiently, and maintain organized server hierarchies.

Built with precision and reliability in mind, Roles offers a comprehensive set of features that make Discord server administration effortless and intuitive.

Auto Roles

Automatically assign up to 10 roles to new members when they join your server. Configure whether to include or exclude bots from receiving these roles.

Reaction Roles

Set up interactive reaction roles on any message with up to 50 configurations per server. Choose from toggle, add-only, remove-only, or reverse modes.

Sticky Roles

Automatically re-assign up to 10 roles to users who previously had them when they rejoin your server. User data is safely stored for 14 days after leaving.

Configuration Management

Easily view all your role configurations with detailed listings and remove specific settings or reset everything when needed. Full control over your server's role automation.

Getting Started

Ready to transform your Discord server management? Adding Roles to your server takes just a few clicks:

  • Click "Add to Server" and select your Discord server
  • Grant the necessary permissions for Roles to function properly
  • Configure your preferred role management settings using our slash commands
  • Start enjoying automated, efficient role management
